Introduction

The College of Engineering Student Chapter (CENG SC), in collaboration with the IEEE IoT Initiative Activities Board is organising a summer workshop to promote and facilitate STEM education among students. Specialised learning modules have been arranged by the CENG SC for S4 to S6 students to cultivate their interest in engineering innovation. The workshop will introduce “Internet of Things” to high school students with a hands-on learning experience. The 3-day workshop will be conducted on-campus from August 11 to August 13, complying with the social distancing measures and facilitated by a team of 2 lecturers and 8 teaching assistants. Each participant will get the opportunity to attend the lectures and laboratory sessions to enrich conceptual understanding and application.

What will the modules cover?

  • Introduction to “Internet of Things”
  • Architecture of IoT systems and Key components
  • Understanding Microcontrollers, Sensors and Actuators
  • Coding with C
  • Experiments on Interfacing and Programming
  • Building an Application
  • Understanding the Cloud
  • Connecting and using Cloud ready IoT servers
  • Building the IoT Car Park Model
